Frequently asked questions

How does Khazen connect to my bank?

Through Plaid, the same secure aggregation service used by Venmo, Robinhood, Chime, and most major US fintech apps. Your bank credentials go directly to Plaid — Khazen never sees them. Plaid then provides Khazen with read-only access to your account balances, transactions, and account details.

Does Khazen store my data on your servers?

No. Your transactions, balances, and budgets live in your iCloud account, synced through Apple's CloudKit. We have no access to your CloudKit container.

The only things we keep server-side are technical tokens needed to fetch your bank data on demand and notify your devices when there are updates. See the Privacy Policy for the full breakdown.

Why do I need a subscription?

Khazen pays Plaid for every bank connection. Without a subscription, we'd either have to limit you to one bank or sell ads — neither of which respects your money or your privacy. The subscription covers the costs and keeps Khazen sustainable for one developer.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, anytime. Go to iOS Settings → your Apple ID → Subscriptions → Khazen and cancel. You keep access until the end of your current billing period. Khazen automatically disconnects your banks on our backend when your subscription ends, so you're never charged for inactive connections.

How does iCloud sync work?

Khazen uses CloudKit, Apple's iCloud-backed sync service. Your data syncs across iPhone, iPad, and Mac when you're signed into the same Apple ID. We don't have access to your iCloud — only Apple does.

You can disable sync from Settings → Data Sync if you prefer device-only storage.

What is the AI assistant? Does it cost extra?

Khazen includes two kinds of AI:

1. On-device categorization using Apple Intelligence — automatic, private, no extra cost. Your transactions never leave your device.

2. Optional financial assistant using Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, or Google Gemini — you provide your own API key (BYOK). Khazen never pays for these on your behalf, and we never see your prompts or your key.

How do I delete all my data?

Settings → Delete My Data. This wipes:

· Local SwiftData database
· iCloud sync container
· All Plaid Items (banks disconnected)
· Your subscription association on our backend

Cancellation of the App Store subscription itself is separate (do that from iOS Settings).

My bank is showing the wrong balance / a transaction is missing.

Pull-to-refresh in the Accounts tab forces a sync from Plaid. If a transaction is still missing after that, it likely hasn't posted at the bank yet — Plaid can only see what your bank has confirmed. Some banks delay posting by 1–3 days.

If a balance is consistently wrong (not just timing), email me with a screenshot and the institution name and I'll investigate.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Email hello@khassinx.com. The more detail the better — screenshots, steps to reproduce, and your iOS version help me fix things faster. There's also a "Report a Problem" button inside the app (Settings → Soporte) that pre-fills useful diagnostic info.
