Money you understand.
Privacy you keep.

Khazen is a personal finance app built for Apple platforms. On-device intelligence, secure bank connections, honest pricing. No advertising, no data brokers, no surprises.

Coming soon to the App Store

Khazen is built by KhassinX, an independent studio for privacy-first Apple platform apps.

How Khazen is different

On-device intelligence

Insights are generated locally by Apple Intelligence — your transactions never leave your iPhone for AI inference. Optional external assistants run via keys you control.

Secure bank connections

Connect your accounts through a regulated financial data network used by major U.S. banks. Tokens are encrypted in transit and at rest. You can disconnect any account at any time.

Apple-native everywhere

iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and Mac. Universal across your devices through your own iCloud account. Built on iOS 26 Liquid Glass — no legacy theme paint.

No advertising. Ever.

We do not sell, share, or monetize your financial data. No third-party analytics. No tracking SDKs. No identity graphs.

Honest pricing

One subscription that explains itself: free for seven days, then a fair monthly or yearly price. Cancel from your Apple ID at any moment, no friction.

Account deletion in one tap

When you decide to leave, your bank connections, transactions, and account are removed from our systems — verifiably and immediately.
